The Nebula Academy — Term I

Pain, Meaning, and The Human Condition

An initiation into seriousness. It does not offer self-improvement or optimism. It teaches attention.

Term I of The Nebula Academy is an initiation into seriousness. Across three modules, participants are invited to confront pain, darkness, and inner conflict — not as problems to be solved, but as realities that reveal what is most true about being human.
