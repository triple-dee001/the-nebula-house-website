Hell Is Empty and All the Devils Are Here

by Kelechi Oji

Jul 7, 2024 · 5 min read

Most of life’s pain and suffering are due to man’s malevolence, greed, and lust for power, money, and all whatnot. We have grown to understand love and hate, malevolence, evil, and wickedness. How did God’s creation become so capable of wickedness and evil? How can slave owners look upon their tinted image and yet commit atrocities against them? How can man develop concentration camps and, within those walls, perpetuate nefarious acts against mankind, as seen in Nazi Germany? How can a political leader watch millions starve to death due to political feuds and power greed? How can Joseph Stalin be born and raised by a woman, yet cause one of the worst famines in Europe, the Holodomor of Ukraine? How can King Leopold II, with all his power and riches in Belgium, still allow greed to find a place in his heart and act it out by killing millions in the DR Congo? All for what?

How can African chiefs sell their own race for weaponry? How can doctors who swore to protect and preserve lives carry out the Tuskegee experiment? How did the events of Unit 731 happen? How did man grow so heartless and without a soul? How can the American government pass into law the killing of children under the disguise of women’s choice? How can we humans save ourselves from ourselves? How can a man seek to satisfy his sexual desire forcefully upon a woman? How can we behold the best man that ever lived, Christ, with so much anger and contempt that we went the extra mile to nail Him on the cross for a sin He did not commit?

Definition. Definition is the answer to the questions above. How we define a thing determines how we approach and treat it. We must be cautious of our definitions. We should have ideologies, but not let ideologies have us. We must observe ourselves, lest we become the monsters we are fighting.

Hitler did not see the Jews as people, as humans. If he did, he would have felt guilt and shame for his actions against them. He saw them as enemies to be killed, enemies of Germany’s progress. Therefore, killing and exterminating the Jews was justified as a higher cause rather than pure wickedness. The same applies to the doctors who carried out the Tuskegee experiment. Black men were not people to them. They were simply lab rats and sub-humans. Once there is an ill definition of a thing, our approach to it reflects such definitions.

For example, most Americans love dogs and treat them well. This pet has a place in the heart of many citizens of the West. The dog is considered part of the family and even of society. However, in parts of Africa, the same dogs are hunted and cooked for dinner. “What!” you may exclaim. The lover of dogs may call such people heartless and without a soul. If you truly love dogs, you may even sue such persons for animal cruelty.

The people who practice the killing and eating of dogs are possibly among the nicest in Africa. How can the nicest people kill and eat such an innocent and “cute” animal? Definition.

Do not be quick to judge, you lover and defender of dogs, for you eat beef. What do Hindus think of you? You are literally eating what they consider sacred, their ancestor, god, or symbol of reverence. To them, you are cruel. You are not, but you get my point.

The moment we define the baby in the womb as a lump of cells, removing such a lump is no longer murder. The moment Black people are considered sub-human, enslaving them becomes no crime to be ashamed of. When Christ is no longer defined as the Son of God, His crucifixion becomes a mere punishment.

We have caused more suffering on earth simply because of definition. The devil gave Eve a definition of the fruit in Genesis 3:1–5. She accepted it, and here we are in this fallen world. We are up against world indoctrination. The media forces you to believe or define things in a certain way. Whatever such definition is, it will greatly affect your approach to it. Scrutinize every ideology. Have an ideology, but do not let it have you.

Hell is empty, and all the devils are here to convince you that we do not need God, the moral lawgiver. Hell is empty, and all the devils are here to tell you that keeping yourself until marriage is absurd and old-fashioned. Hell is empty, and all the devils are here to tell you that hating men is the right thing to do for the sake of gender equality. Hell is empty, and all the devils are here to tell you that respecting and honoring the family structure is ludicrous. Hell is empty, and all the devils are here telling young men that sexual immorality is acceptable. Hell is empty, and all the devils are here convincing women that their bodies are products to be sold for fame and money.

Hell is empty, and all the devils are here convincing us that hating each other because of political and cultural differences is fine and acceptable.

Hell is empty, and all the devils are here.

“So humble yourselves before God, resist the devil, and he will flee from you.” — James the Apostle
